Description

Marbled Salamanders are medium sized salamanders with a notably robust body. Although they have not been officially recorded in VT, their range ends just below the state. As adults, they have dorsal white marbled marks that can sometimes appear greyish on females. Their breeding season is during fall, where they will journey to vernal pools in search of a mate and a suitable place to lay eggs.
